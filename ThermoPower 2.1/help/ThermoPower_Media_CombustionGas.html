ThermoPower.Media.CombustionGas

Mixture of O2, H2O, CO2, N2, CH4

Information


This Medium is a mixture of O2, H2O, CO2, N2, CH4. It has its reference composition, defined as Medium.reference_X, but only changing it, it can be reused as Air, just leading to zero the mass fraction of CH4, and as Fuel keeping only the mass fraction of CH4, as the unique gas.
